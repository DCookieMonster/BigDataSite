DMBI

The Annual DM for BI conference, organized by the Department of Software and Information Systems Engineering ​at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, aims at bringing together researchers and practitioners in data mining, data science, machine learning, predictive analytics and related fields to discuss emerging trends and key issues in utilization of data mining methods for business intelligence.
